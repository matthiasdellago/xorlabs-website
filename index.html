XOR Labs

XOR Labs is establishing a principled understanding of how agentic behaviour emerges in AI systems, what capabilities and risks result from this, and how these risks can be mitigated. We develop formal tools to characterize the emergence of agency in individual AI systems, and extend these tools to interacting agents in order to identify emergent collective capabilities and failure modes.
